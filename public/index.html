Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tastronaut.

Is Tastronaut really free?

Yes! Tastronaut offers 12 destinations with 60+ recipes completely free, forever. Unlock all 237 destinations and 1,185+ recipes with a one-time purchase—no subscriptions.

What kind of recipes are included?

Each destination features 5 authentic recipes: a national dish, street food, home cooking, a holiday specialty, and a dessert. All recipes include step-by-step instructions and dietary substitutions.

Can I use Tastronaut offline?

Yes! Once you've loaded a recipe, it's available offline. Perfect for cooking without interruptions or when you're traveling.

What dietary options are supported?

Every recipe includes substitutions for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other dietary needs. Cook dishes that fit your lifestyle.
